Chapter 10: The Demon King’s Fortress

The rebellion, bolstered by Kaelen’s increasing strength, prepares for the final assault on Zorath’s fortress. Zorath’s citadel, a towering spire of black stone, is surrounded by a vast army of demons and dark creatures. The fortress is nearly impenetrable, and the rebels know that this will be their hardest challenge yet.

the rebel forces launch their assault, Kaelen leads the charge, wielding the Angel’s Blade with confidence. However, Zorath’s fortress is not just a physical stronghold; it is a manifestation of Zorath’s dark magic. The closer Kaelen gets to the heart of the fortress, the more he feels the pull of darkness, as if the very walls are trying to corrupt him. His angelic powers are tested to their limits as he faces an army of demonic forces in one of the largest battles the world has ever seen.

Throughout the chapter, Kaelen's leadership skills are tested. While Lira and Eldric lead the front lines, Kaelen must contend with his inner battle, resisting the seductive pull of his past and the growing doubts that his mission might lead to his own fall. The siege against the fortress is brutal, and many rebel lives are lost. But Kaelen’s resolve remains unshaken, though the physical and emotional toll weighs heavily on him. The chapter ends with Kaelen and his allies breaching the fortress gates, setting the stage for the final confrontation with Zorath.
